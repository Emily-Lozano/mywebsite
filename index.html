FRC 2025 Reefscape

Here is the drafted KrayonCad that I contributed on for the FRC robotics team at my school for 2025. The game for the competition this year was recently released and my team held a two day kickofff in which we discussed game strategies and robot designs. After a long discussion we made this very preminalary first design using KrayonCad on onshape. The design we hope to achieve for this year consists of four major components. These components are a three staged cascade elevator, a reoriening mechanism for the cylindrical allow it to be reoriented to the vertical axis, a cylinder (coral) specific intake, a ball (algae) specific intake which consists of a roller.
